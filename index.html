Welcome

Almost twenty years back I created my first web page and that was with Yahoo Geo Cities. I fell in love with the process of creating a web page and deploying that. There was no social media and Orkut came a bit later. I used to visit the webpages of many scientists and most of them used to be very primitive and webpages of the well know orgnaizations were also not fancy. I wrote my own html/css and created framed, side/top bars, embedded images and even calanders. A list of useful resources also has been an integral part of the webpage. Later my host institute provided me and other members dedicated webspace - any content which we put in our public_html folder will go live next day ! Ok, let me first tell something about myself.

In 2001 I joined Harish-Chandra Research Institute in Allahabad (now Pryagraj) as a Ph.D student. After compleating almost two years long course work I decided to go for Astronomy & Astrophyics (Cosmology) for my Ph.D thesis.
